h1. textile-epubs

This is a small repository with free ePub documents that I want to share with anyone who is interested on them.

The source documents will be in the "Textile lightweight format":http://www.txstyle.org.

h2. License

Unless stated otherwise, files are released under a "Creative Commons Attribution--ShareAlike":https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/3.0/deed.en_US license.

Please, don't forget that even public domain texts are protected by _[la]sui generis_ database rights in the European Union. Please, check your local law to comply with the license when required.
